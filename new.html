Hello and welcome!

I was born in CA, I have lived in 21 different countries and 3 different states. I currently live in TN, about 50 minutes outside of Nashville. I originally went to school to become a Respiratory Care Practitioner. I worked in the ER, Trauma, ICU and burn unit for 10+ years. Basically, if your were coding(not what we are doing), I was there!

I decided to get out of the medical field and I almost became a web developer 7 years ago, but chose to start my business instead. I started my first business calledLucky Dogabout 7 years ago. I sold that business to buy a home here in TN. I've been dog training, boarding and walking ever since.

When I am not working you will find me outside exploring, hiking, paddleboarding, camping. If the weather is terrible and I do mean terrible then I will be inside playing games, playing piano, writing or cuddling with my boyfriend and pups.

I got into coding 7 year ago and did some html, css and javascript courses on Kahn Academy. I was very fascinated with it and intended to continue learning it, but my business took off. Now, with my new business, I mainly do the dog training and administrative work so I have time to focus on becoming a web developer.
